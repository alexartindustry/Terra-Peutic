PERU

Day 1

Indulge in comfort and sophistication as you depart on your overnight flight to Lima. Elevate your travel experience with unparalleled service, exquisite cuisine, and plush seating, ensuring your journey is as memorable as the destination. Welcome to a seamless beginning of your deluxe escapade into the heart of Peru.

Day 2

Upon landing in Lima, the gastronomic capital of the Americas, you will be greeted by our TerraPeutic representative and transferred to your hotel. In the evening, we celebrate the beginning of our journey with a welcome dinner at a renowned local restaurant, offering a taste of Peru's exquisite culinary landscape.

Day 3

After breakfast, we delve into Lima's rich history, starting with the city's heart, the Plaza Mayor. As we explore the Cathedral and San Francisco Monastery, each stone and artifact whispers stories of Lima's colonial past.

In the afternoon, we shift gears to experience modern Lima. We taste the city's contemporary culture by exploring districts like Miraflores and Barranco. The day is rounded off with a gastronomic experience, sampling Peruvian cuisine at a top local restaurant.

Day 4

We leave behind the coastal beauty of Lima for the enigmatic Sacred Valley. After landing in Cusco, we immediately head to the valley, stopping at fascinating Inca ruins and local markets. This realm is where ancient civilization and natural beauty coalesce in timeless harmony.

Day 5

After breakfast, we ascend to the fortress of Ollantaytambo, an impressive construction with steep terraces guarding the Inca citadel. In the afternoon, we lose ourselves in the Pisac Market's colors, textures, and crafts, where tradition comes alive in every stall.

Day 6

We board the train to Aguas Calientes, the gateway to Machu Picchu. After settling into our hotel, we ascend the mountain and spend the afternoon exploring the iconic Inca ruins, a spiritual sanctuary suspended between Heaven and Earth.

Day 7

After breakfast, enjoy some free time to explore Machu Picchu further or relax in Aguas Calientes. In the afternoon, we board the train back to Cusco, where the city's charming blend of Inca and colonial architecture awaits us.

Day 8

We begin our day by visiting the salt pans of Maras, a surreal landscape resembling a white labyrinth. Next, we head to Moray, an Inca archaeological site featuring terraced circular depressions. In the afternoon, we visit an Alpaca farm to learn about this vital Andean animal and the luxurious textiles it produces.

Day 9
Cusco city tour: Qoricancha Inca Temple, Cusco Cathedral, Sacsayhuama & Tribute to Mother Earth in Cusco

The morning sun greets us as we prepare for a day rich in exploration and profound spiritual experience. Our footsteps lead us to the illustrious Koricancha, once the most important temple in the Inca Empire. Here, we will feel the weight of centuries as we explore its golden walls, standing as they have for countless generations. Leaving behind the gleaming treasure of Koricancha, we move to the main square, where the imposing Cathedral awaits our arrival. An architectural masterpiece, the Cathedral is not just a house of worship but also a living museum filled with sacred art that tells the layered histories of this ancient city. We ascend to Sacsayhuamán, the fortress with walls that interlock precisely and defy time. A powerful testament to Inca engineering, the stone colossus leaves an indelible impression of awe.

We break for a sumptuous grill lunch, feasting on locally sourced delights prepared over an open flame, a perfect mingling of tradition and modern gastronomy.

The evening brings different sustenance as we engage in a Tribute to Mother Earth, or "Pachamama." We participate in an age-old ceremony, guided by a local shaman, to express our gratitude to Earth for its endless generosity.

Day 10
CUSCO/ DEPARTURE

We wake to our final morning in this land of wonders. After a leisurely breakfast, the morning is yours to explore Cusco one last time, perhaps to pick up some souvenirs or to soak in the atmosphere of this unique city.

In the afternoon, a private transfer will take us to Cusco’s airport, where we will prepare for our international flights home. As the airplane lifts off, the captivating landscape of Peru slips away beneath us, but the experiences and memories we have gathered will remain treasured keepsakes from an incredible journey.
